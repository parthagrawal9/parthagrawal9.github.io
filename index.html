About Us

Welcome To Silver Ride Trading

Products & Services

Food Stuffs, fruits and vegetable supplies

We are a global importer and supplier of wide variety of rice, spices, and pulses for the retail, industrial, and food service markets. Rice being a major food, we ensure that people get various quality of rice depending on choice of consumption and affordability. We have different types of other cereals and grains mainly for human consumption. All the foods and beverages we are dealing are of the finest quality and all of them undergo a quality check before packaging which is 100% safe. Company is dealing with various pules and spices by importing from various countries. The division also supplies fruits & Vegetables to a large number of Catering companies, restaurants, Food Suppliers, Supermarkets & Hypermarkets in the country on wholesale and retail basis.

Restaurants & Catering Services

The Company owns restaurants and fully capable of serving breakfast, meals , dinner and many food items to the customers, camps, sites or any functions. Fully air conditioned trucks and hot cases will deliver the food with high quality and in hygienic way to the clients. Party hall is available with all the facilities to arrange any parties up to the number of 100 people. Restaurant serves South Indian and North Indian Foods along with Arabic foods in dine facility and for catering needs. Whatever be your catering needs, we are ready. Monthly mess facilities for executive bachelors are also provided.

Tissue Boxes, Maxi Rolls & disposable food packing materials

The absorbent disposable paper which work efficiently towards making our face fresh and clean with a single wipe. Whether it be sweaty outdoor experiences or messy encounters, facial tissues are a must to maintain the beauty and elegance of those charming faces. Our range of tissues which are premium in quality, soft and hygienic and made out of 100% virgin cellulose will certainly be a great choice for your delicate skin. Maxi rolls, toilet tissues are other major products from out distribution net work.

Lubricants

Seeing the demand in the increase of vehicles, company started dealing with trading of automotive oil in the market. With the name German Gold, company presently dealing with petrol engine oils, company has plans to trade in all the types of lubricants in the near future.

Pure Drinking Water

As we all know, Water is a great way to eliminate dehydration and all of its related problems. Hence to provide high quality of drinking water to the people, the Company is distributing pure packaged water in the sizes of 200 ml, 350 ml, 500 ml and 1.5 litres and 5 gallons. With ISO certification for the production, the company is maintaining low sodium and high quality.

Washing , Cleaning chemicals & materials

Company imports quality chemicals from Turkey for the use in houses, restaurants, industrial cleaning Etc. Company also provides washing shampoo and materials for laundry, car wash etc.

Stationary & Printing supplies

Silver Ride distributes no. 1 quality of photo copy papers in low prices to meet the local demand of the companies, shops, schools and offices without any compromise in the quality. The items include pens, papers, files, cartridges, etc. on wholesale and retail basis.

Transportation & Material supplies

With own fleet of 22 trucks, company undertakes the contract of transportation, supply of tankers, equipments and vehicles on monthly rental contract. Supply of materials like gabbro, fill materials, dune sand and washed sand are also available.

Buy & Sell used cars

Company buys used cars and resell to the valued customers. Latest and old model cars, pick ups and vehicles are dealt with. You can start your search, and get the best deals for used cars for sale where we have massive selection of affordable used cars for sale by brand.
